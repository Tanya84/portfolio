Isometrics Illustration

Overview

I conceived a concept for an isometric illustration. The criteria was that the theme must be obvious to a majority of people, and the illustration needed to explain a process. This was my fist experience ever creating this type of illustration, the theme I came up with was acrtic exploration because i'm in love with the arctic especially it's wild life.
