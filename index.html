About Me

Heyo! Tanya here, and I am yes you guessed it, a Graphic designer! Ever since I was able to pick up a pencil i’ve always loved to draw, it was something that just came to me naturally. Whenever I was asked as a kid what I wanted to be when I grew up I always was quick to shout out an Artist! I was often told from a young age that there was no future in the arts, and unfortunately for many years those influences led me down the wrong path. I’ve always been a creative person with a fierce love for the arts so one day I decided to take a chance on myself and as corny as this sounds to follow a dream. I think that is a huge part of why I love being a graphic designer, because in a way it’s making a clients dream a reality.
